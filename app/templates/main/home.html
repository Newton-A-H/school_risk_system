How it works

The system supports a more natural school workflow instead of forcing everything into one page.

Learner intake comes first, then academic records, then questionnaire input, and finally prediction and intervention support.

Built for real school use

EduSentinel AI is structured for practical use across departments, not just as a one-off academic demo.

It separates users by role, keeps records organised, and helps reduce manual follow-up work.

Support when it matters

A risk alert alone is not enough. The system is designed to pair prediction with meaningful action.

That includes recommendation summaries, urgency levels, and space for lecturer verification and school follow-up.

Need access to the platform?

New students and lecturers can request an account, verify their email, and wait for administrator approval. The system is designed to create and link the right records automatically after approval.
