How it works

The system follows the way schools already work instead of squeezing everything into one screen.

First the learner record is added, then marks and attendance, then the student check-in, and finally the prediction and follow-up.

Built for real school use

EduSentinel AI is set up for day-to-day school use, not just as a one-time academic project.

It separates users by role, keeps records organised, and cuts down on manual follow-up.

Support when it matters

A warning on its own is not enough. The system is meant to connect risk with real action.

That includes suggested next steps, urgency levels, and room for lecturer review and follow-up.

Need access to the platform?

New students and lecturers can ask for an account, confirm their email, and wait for approval. Once approved, the right account details and record links are set up automatically.
